Activities

Advocacies/Campaigns

Routine inspections and monitoring of the Engineering Department's facilities and laboratories are among the main objectives of ENsured Quality. The council will quickly identify any possible problem in this way to ensure that corrective measures are taken. Students must be comfortable in their learning environment. Therefore, a proactive approach is necessary with regards to ensuring that they have access to the best resources available for their educational needs, that will enhance academic performance and enrich the overall learning experience.

The continuous fight against Tuition and Other Fees Increase (TOFI) aims to ensure every student's right to access affordable and quality education. Through a variety of movements, empowering actions, and stronger measures, we can magnify our voices and push for the administration to halt TOFI. Through unity and empathy, the campaign endeavors to uphold the relevant and affordable quality education.

The State of the Student Address serves as a formal congress for communication and dialogue between the student body and college officials. These discussions encompass academic policies, campus facilities, student services, and more. The assembly serves as an avenue for students to voice their concerns, propose initiatives, and advocate for changes. Through this collaborative exchange, we strive to ensure that students receive their rightful entitlements, urging the administration to hear our plea and address our concerns. This fosters open communication and empowers students to take charge of their educational experience, actively contributing to the betterment of our college.

ENspacio aims to create additional learning environments to maximize educational resources. Through careful assessment and redesign, these underutilized rooms will be transformed into multifunctional hubs for collaboration, creativity, and engagement.

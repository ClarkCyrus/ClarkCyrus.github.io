How To Cast Your Vote

1. LOG IN to your CANVAS ACCOUNT.

2. Locate the ELECTION COURSE on your DASHBOARD.

3. Follow the instructions found in the INTRODUCTORY PAGE. You will be prompted with multiple survey instruments to capture your votes.

4. Move through each survey instrument by clicking on the NEXT BUTTON.

5. For each survey instrument, click on the SUBMIT BUTTON to vote.

6. FINAL MESSAGE will appear to signal the end of the survey.

7. LOG OUT of your Canvas account.

8. DO NOT SHARE to anyone who you have voted for. YOUR VOTE IS SACRED.
